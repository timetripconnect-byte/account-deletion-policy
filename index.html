Account Deletion Policy

At Time Trip, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. Learn how to delete your account and what happens to your data.

Last Updated: [Add Date]

By using Time Trip, you agree to this Account Deletion Policy.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can delete your account using:

  • From within the app: Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Or contact us at: support@timetrip.com

What Happens When You Delete

When you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Personal info (name, email, phone, profile) will be removed
  • Your matches, groups, and interactions will be deleted
  • Authentication data (tokens, sessions) will be invalidated

Data Retention

We may retain limited data for a short period:

  • Backup data retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion
  • Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention purposes
  • Security purposes

Third-Party Services

If you signed in using third-party services (such as Google or Facebook), disconnecting from those services does not automatically delete your Time Trip account. You must follow the deletion process described above.

Processing Time

  • Deletion requests are processed within 7 days
  • In some cases, it may take up to 30 days to completely remove all data from backups

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Account Deletion Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Your data, your control

TimeTrip is committed to giving you full control over your personal data. We make deletion simple and transparent.
